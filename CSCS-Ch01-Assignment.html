Chapter 1

Assignment

Three tasks to test your understanding of muscle anatomy, sarcomere mechanics, and the cardiovascular system. Complete them on paper or digitally, compile into one PDF, and submit below.

Assignment 1 of 3
Muscle Connective Tissue Layers
Draw a cross-section of a whole muscle and zoom into its layers. Show the outer sheath that wraps the entire muscle, the layer that surrounds each bundle of fibres inside, and the thin wrapping around each individual muscle fibre. Label all three layers clearly with their correct names.
Refer to Figure 1.3 — NSCA 5th Edition
Assignment 2 of 3
The Sarcomere at Three Lengths
Draw the sarcomere side by side in three states — fully stretched, partially contracted (the sweet spot), and fully compressed. For each state, show the I-bands and H-zone and indicate whether force potential is high or low and why. Label all key zones.
Refer to Figure 1.7 — NSCA 5th Edition
Assignment 3 of 3
The Heart and Blood Flow
Draw the human heart showing all four chambers. Use arrows to trace the path blood takes through the heart — indicate which side handles the lungs (pulmonary) and which side handles the rest of the body (systemic). Label the chambers and major vessels.
Refer to Figure 1.11 — NSCA 5th Edition
How to submit: Complete all three drawings, scan or photograph them (or export from your drawing app), and combine into a single PDF. Maximum file size is 25 MB. If your file is larger, compress it first using smallpdf.com or ilovepdf.com before uploading.
