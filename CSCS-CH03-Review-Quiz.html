Ranjit Nahak, MSc, CSCS
Bioenergetics is the engine room of everything we do as coaches. If you know which system is being stressed and why, your programme design becomes intentional rather than guesswork. Review any domain below 60% — especially the oxidative system and metabolic specificity sections, as these are heavily tested on the CSCS exam. Go back to the mechanisms, not just the labels. Work-to-rest ratios, ATP yield per pathway, and lactate thresholds are high-priority exam topics.
